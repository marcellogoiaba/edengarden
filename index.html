Eden Garden
A Tradition of fine food & drink.

Quality Locally Produced Products and Suppliers wherever possible.

At Eden Garden we are committed to quality, that's why we bake our own breads. We partner with companies who are as passionate about what they do as we are. We believe in fresh and local.

Location

Eden Garden is just 5 minutes drive from Galway city centre. We have a 12 room boutique hotel with a popular bar and restaurant, overlooking the shores of Lough Atalia.

Rooms & amenities

Our rooms are modern and offer tea and coffee making facilities and a TV with a DVD player, along with a hairdryer and ironing supplies. During your stay at Eden Garden we offer free Wi-Fi and free private parking.

Facilities

The spacious tiled en suite shower rooms offer complimentary toiletries. In the morning enjoy an extensive breakfast menu, which includes full Irish breakfast.

Bar & Restaurant

We don't limit your creativity, which means you can order whatever you feel like. You can also choose from our menu containing over 100 delicious meals. It's up to you!

Quality local suppliers and produce a priority at Eden Garden.

We strive to keep our menu fresh, inviting and innovative. We source foods locally where ever possible. We watch trends and constantly adjust our menu to cater for the demands of our customers. Whether you want a traditional beer battered fish and chips or Mediterranean style platters we have sourced the ingredients from the best possible sources. All our scones, breads and desserts are baked freshly in house everyday.
